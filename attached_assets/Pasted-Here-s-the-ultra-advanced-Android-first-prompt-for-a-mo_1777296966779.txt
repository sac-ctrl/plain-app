1. CORE GAMEPLAY & PHYSICS (Pixel‑perfect precision)

· Basic setup –
  · Targets appear on screen at random or predetermined patterns.
  · Tap / drag / flick to hit (depending on mode).
  · No crosshair by default (uses touch point), optional dot crosshair.
· Target types –
  · Static – circles, heads, diamonds.
  · Moving – linear, sinusoidal, circular, random walk.
  · Flying – birds, drones, projectiles.
  · Peek – appear briefly then hide.
  · Burst – multiple targets in quick succession.
· Hit detection – Within target radius (configurable size).
· Scoring –
  · Accuracy = hits / shots.
  · Reaction time = tap delay from target spawn.
  · Combo = consecutive hits.
  · Headshot bonus = centre 20% of target.
· Weapon simulation (optional modes) –
  · Hitscan (instant).
  · Projectile (bullet travel time with gravity).
  · Shotgun (spread).

---

2. INPUT & SENSITIVITY (Zero‑latency tapping)

· Primary control – Tap anywhere on target area.
  · For precision modes: tap within target radius.
  · For tracking: drag along moving target (must stay inside).
· Sensitivity settings –
  · Tap radius – how close to centre counts as hit (loose to strict).
  · Dead zone – area near screen edges ignores taps.
  · Touch prediction – optional (estimates tap offset from movement).
· Haptics –
  · Light tap on hit.
  · Medium buzz on headshot / perfect.
  · Strong buzz on miss.
  · Vibration burst on new high score.
· Click vs drag mode – Switch between tap‑only and hold‑for‑continuous (e.g., auto rifle).
· Input filtering – Reject accidental palm touches (edge detection).

3. MODERN VISUALS & ENVIRONMENT (Clean, competitive)

Targets & hit feedback

· Targets: glowing, soft shadows, pulsing on spawn.
· Hit effect: impact splash, crosshair flash, number pop‑up (+10, HEADSHOT).
· Miss effect: red “X”, screen edge pulse (optional).
· Death animation: target shatters or fades with particle trail.

Backgrounds & themes

· Minimal – solid colour, grid, or low‑contrast gradient (reduces distraction).
· Arenas – Unlockable:
  · Training room (grey walls, mats).
  · Neon grid (cyber).
  · Zen garden (calm).
  · Warzone (smoke, debris).
· Crosshair overlay – customisable dot, circle, plus, dynamic (expands on hit).

Performance overlays

· Real‑time stats: accuracy, reaction time, hits/misses, combo.
· Heatmap overlay (post‑run) – where you tap most on screen.
· Audio visualiser for shots (optional).

4. PROGRESSION & DIFFICULTY ECOSYSTEM

Training modes (extensive)

1. Flick training – static targets, random positions.
2. Tracking – moving target (smooth or erratic).
3. Switch – multiple sequential targets.
4. Reaction – targets appear for 150–500ms only.
5. Sniper – small targets, long distance (pinch to zoom).
6. Overwatch – targets hidden behind moving obstacles.
7. Custom – set size, speed, pattern, duration.

Adaptive difficulty

· After 5 consecutive hits → increase speed or decrease target size.
· After 3 misses → temporarily slow down or enlarge next target.
· Personalised difficulty curve based on average reaction time (keeps 70–80% accuracy).

Meta‑progression & unlocks

· Weapon skins – laser, bullet trails, impact effects.
· Target skins – classic, neon, cartoon, realistic.
· Arenas – unlock with points.
· Stat upgrades (cosmetic only – no P2W):
  · Highlight enemy for longer (visual only).
  · Audio cues for perfect hit.
· Rank system – Iron → Bronze → … → Radiant based on ELO across modes.

Daily & weekly challenges

· Fixed seed target sequences – same for all players.
· Leaderboards: highest accuracy, fastest average RT, longest combo.
