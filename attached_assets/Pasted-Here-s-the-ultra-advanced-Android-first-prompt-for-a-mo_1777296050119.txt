Here’s the ultra‑advanced, Android‑first prompt for a modern Lane Rush (highway dodging / endless runner – think Subway Surfers meets Dash but with lane‑based precision).

---

🛣️ Game Title: Lane Rush: Velocity Shift

Core Vibe: Blazing fast, one‑thumb perfection. Every lane change is a life‑or‑death flick.

---

1. CORE GAMEPLAY & PHYSICS (Instant reaction)

· Basic setup –
  · 3, 4, or 5 lanes (adjustable in settings).
  · Player automatically moves forward at increasing speed.
  · Tap / swipe left/right to change lanes instantly (no acceleration curve).
· Obstacles – Cars, barriers, roadblocks, moving objects.
  · Some stay in one lane; others weave between lanes.
· Collision – Pixel‑perfect hitbox; 1 hit = run ends (with shield power‑up as exception).
· Speed curve – Starts at comfortable pace, ramps exponentially until impossible (endless mode).
· Pickups –
  · Shield (1 free hit).
  · Slow motion (2 sec).
  · Coin magnet.
  · Double score for 5 sec.
· Score system – Points per distance + per overtaken vehicle + combo for consecutive near misses.

---

2. INPUT & SENSITIVITY (Swipe perfection)

· Primary controls –
  · Swipe left/right on bottom half of screen → lane change.
  · Tap left/right (accessibility alternative).
  · Tilt (gyro) – optional, sensitivity slider.
· Sensitivity –
  · Swipe length threshold (short flick vs long drag).
  · Dead zone in centre to avoid accidental lane changes.
· Haptics –
  · Light tick on lane change.
  · Medium burst on near miss (car passes close).
  · Strong buzz on collision.
  · Soft pulse on pickup.
· Buffering – If you swipe during a lane change animation (instant by default), it queues the next change (max 1 buffer).
· Edge protection – Cannot swipe beyond leftmost/rightmost lane.

---

3. MODERN VISUALS & ENVIRONMENT (Highway fantasy)

Player vehicle

· Sports car with customisable skins, neon underglow, glowing trails.
· Damage effect on hit – sparks, then crumple + slow‑mo explosion.

Lanes & road

· 3D perspective with smooth horizon.
· Dynamic road textures: asphalt, rain (reflective), night (streetlights), desert, neon city.
· Lane markings – white dashed lines that animate toward camera.
· Weather & time – Day, sunset, night, rain, fog (cycles every 5 runs).

Obstacles (20+ types)

· Standard cars, trucks, police cars (harder hitbox), motorcycles (fast lane changers).
· Moving obstacles – zigzag drones, rolling barrels.
· Elite – glowing red enemies that follow your lane.
· Boss events – every 2000m, a truck blockades all lanes; must fit through moving gap.

Effects & juice

· Speed lines on sides when over 80% max speed.
· Near miss: screen edge glow + time dilation (very subtle 0.95x for 0.1s).
· Pickup collection: particle burst, floating text.
· Death: camera shake, slow‑mo 0.3x for 0.5s, vehicle flips.

---

4. PROGRESSION & DIFFICULTY ECOSYSTEM

Game modes

1. Endless – Classic, speed increases forever.
2. Stage rush – Fixed distance levels (e.g., 500m, 1000m, 2000m) with checkpoints.
3. Time trial – 60 seconds, most distance wins.
4. Mission mode – Daily objectives (“Overtake 20 cars”, “Collect 5 shields”).

Adaptive difficulty (keeps it intense but fair)

· After 3 early deaths (<500m) → slower initial speed ramp.
· After 3 long runs (>2000m) → introduce moving obstacles earlier.
· Based on player reaction time (measured from obstacle spawn to lane change), adjust warning time.

Meta‑progression & unlocks

· Vehicles – 20+ cars (stats: handling, visual trail, special ability on long combo).
· Upgrades (earn coins per run):
  · Shield duration, magnet range, slow‑motion length, double score duration.
· Character / driver – skins, voice lines on near miss.
· Road themes – unlockable via achievements.

Daily & weekly challenges

· Fixed seed road layouts – same obstacles for all players.
· Leaderboards: fastest to 2000m, longest survival, most pickups.

---

5. AUDIO & ATMOSPHERE (Adaptive & immersive)

· Music – Electronic / synthwave with tempo that matches game speed (higher speed = faster BPM).
  · Layers: bassline, drums, leads – intensity tied to score multiplier.
· SFX –
  · Lane change: soft whoosh.
  · Near miss: quick sting + adrenaline pulse.
  · Pickup: ascending chime.
  · Collision: deep crunch, tire screech, glass break.
· Voice announcer (toggle) – “Near miss!”, “Shield active”, “Boss incoming”, “New best”.
· Audio ducking – Lowers background music when notifications play.

---

6. ACCESSIBILITY & COMFORT (Android first)

· Colorblind modes – 3 presets (recolour obstacles, pickups, lane markers).
· Reduced motion – Disables screen shake, slow‑mo, speed lines, particles.
· High contrast – Bold outlines for all obstacles.
· Assist modes –
  · Auto‑slow on approach (game slows when obstacle is close).
  · Lane highlight – current lane glows.
  · Infinite shields (practice mode).
· One‑handed mode – Swipe zone limited to right or left half, UI shifted.
· Font scaling – Respects system settings.
· Battery saver – Limit 30fps, remove reflections, reduce particles.

---

7. ANALYTICS & FEEDBACK LOOPS

· Post‑run screen –
  · Lane change heatmap (which lanes you used most).
  · Reaction time graph: delay between obstacle spawn and your lane change.
  · Most deadly obstacle type.
  · Tip: “You hesitated before trucks – try reacting 0.2s earlier.”
· Live sensitivity calibration – “Swipe test” – perform 5 lane changes, game sets threshold.
· Replay system – Watch last run with camera behind car.
· Crash log – Exact frame: obstacle ID, your lane, input received.

---

8. SOCIAL & REPLAYABILITY (Android integration)

· Google Play Games – Achievements, leaderboards, cloud saves.
· Ghost runs – Race your best run (transparent car).
· Share replay – Export as MP4 with speedometer overlay.
· Friend challenges – Send QR code to beat your score on same seed.
· Clubs – Weekly club distance leaderboard, rewards.
· Daily login streak – Bonus coins, exclusive skins.

---

9. TECHNICAL STANDARDS (Android performance)

· Engine – Unity / Godot (Vulkan / OpenGL ES 3.1).
· Frame rate – 60/90/120/144Hz support; game logic at 60Hz fixed, rendering at device refresh.
· Touch latency – <20ms from swipe to lane change.
· Memory – <200 MB (streaming assets for music / themes).
· Battery efficiency – Pause when game is background; dynamic resolution scaling.
· Install size – Under 150 MB (optional HD assets download).
· Permissions – None (gyro optional).
· Offline – Full offline play; sync when online.
