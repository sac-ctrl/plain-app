Here’s the complete, ultra‑advanced prompt for a modern Snake Game – designed specifically for Android mobile (touch‑first, haptics, adaptive layouts, gestures). Same depth as Flappy Eclipse and Crusher: Neon Fracture.

---

🐍 Game Title: Viper: Evolved

Core Vibe: Smooth, hypnotic, and responsive. The snake bends like liquid, and every move feels deliberate.

---

1. CORE GAMEPLAY & PHYSICS (Mobile‑first)

· Grid vs. free‑movement – Two modes (selectable):
  · Classic grid (20x12 cells) – discrete steps.
  · Smooth vector – snake moves in continuous 2D space, rounded collision radius.
· Snake body – Each segment follows the head with lag‑interpolated positions (no rigid blockiness unless in grid mode).
· Movement speed – Adjustable base speed (slow / normal / fast) + power‑ups that temporarily boost speed.
· Turning – Instant response on swipe. No buffered queue (modern: last swipe within 60ms overrides previous).
· Collision rules –
  · Self‑collision: fail unless “phase‑through” power‑up active.
  · Wall collision: fail by default, but “wrap‑around” mode (optional) lets snake exit left/right, top/bottom.
· Food types –
  · Normal (grow +1).
  · Golden (grow +3, speed burst).
  · Poison (shrink or reverse controls).
  · Time‑based (food expires after 5 sec, moves to new location).
· Score multiplier – Consecutive food eats without hitting anything = combo multiplier (up to 5x).

---

2. INPUT & SENSITIVITY (Swipe perfection)

· Primary control – Swipe anywhere on screen (up/down/left/right).
  · Sensitivity slider: low = long swipe needed, high = very short flick triggers turn.
  · Dead zone in centre (optional) for resting thumb.
· Alternative controls –
  · Tilt (gyro) – tilt phone to steer (left/right only, forward fixed).
  · On‑screen directional pad (for accessibility).
· Haptic feedback –
  · Light tap on food collection.
  · Medium buzz on power‑up grab.
  · Double buzz on death.
· Edge‑protection turn limit – Cannot turn 180° into itself (prevent instant suicide).
· Pause on accidental background – Game auto‑pauses if you switch apps or cover proximity sensor (customisable).

---

3. MODERN VISUALS & ENVIRONMENT (OLED‑ready)

Snake design

· Head – Distinct shape (viper wedge) with glowing eyes and subtle idle animation.
· Body – Smooth gradient (head = bright, tail = dark) or neon tube segments.
· Scales / texture – Optional subtle bump map or holographic shimmer.
· Tail whip – Last segment leaves a fading particle trail.
· Death animation – Snake shatters into glowing scales, screen shake, slow‑mo.

Arena & backgrounds

· Dynamic themes – Unlockable backgrounds:
  · Cyber grid – pulsing neon lines.
  · Zen garden – sand ripples that react to snake movement.
  · Toxic waste – bubbling green pools.
  · Void – pure black with only snake and food (battery saver).
· Parallax floor – Subtle moving pattern under the snake.
· Reactive lighting – Food emits soft glow; snake’s eyes illuminate nearby area.

Food & power‑up visuals

· 3D‑style orbs with floating animation.
· Golden food – spinning, particles, aura.
· Poison – purple bubbling, skull icon.
· Magnetic food – slowly drifts toward snake (rare).

Effects & juice

· On eat: screen flash (optional), ripple from head position, number pop‑up (+points).
· On combo: screen edge pulsing, trail turns gold, music pitch shift.
· High score celebration – confetti + camera zoom out + slow zoom in.

---

4. PROGRESSION & DIFFICULTY ECOSYSTEM

Adaptive difficulty (mobile retention)

· After 3 deaths below 10 food → slower initial speed, bigger food hitbox.
· After 3 wins above 30 food → add moving obstacles or shrinking arena.
· AI‑driven difficulty – Spawns poison more often when player is doing too well.

Game modes

1. Classic – Endless, lives = 1, score = food eaten.
2. Time attack – 60 seconds, each food adds +2 sec.
3. Maze mode – Static walls and gates that open/close.
4. Battle – Two snakes on same device (split screen) or online (turn‑based or real‑time).
5. Daily maze – New fixed level every day, global leaderboard.

Unlockables & meta‑progression

· Snake skins – Unlocked via achievements:
  · Neon, Crystal, Lava, Ice, Ghost (semi‑transparent), Pixel, Golden.
· Trail effects – Sparkles, fire, smoke, rainbow, none.
· Arenas – Purchase with in‑game coins earned by playing.
· Power‑up upgrades – Magnetic range, golden food chance, etc.

Daily rewards & streaks

· Login bonus, first‑win‑of‑day multiplier.

---

5. AUDIO & ATMOSPHERE (Mobile‑optimised)

· Adaptive music – Dynamic layers:
  · Calm (0–10 food) → added bassline (10–30) → percussion + leads (30+).
  · Death → music drops to low‑pass filter, then rebuilds.
· SFX –
  · Eat: soft crunch + ascending note.
  · Power‑up: arcade chime.
  · Death: deep “whoosh” + crack.
  · Turn swipe: subtle tick (optional).
· Voice hints (disabled by default) – “Nice combo”, “Poison nearby”, “New high score!”
· Audio focus – Duck other apps’ audio appropriately.

---

6. ACCESSIBILITY & COMFORT (Android specific)

· Colorblind modes – 3 presets (red‑green, blue‑yellow, full monochrome).
· Reduced motion – Disables screen shake, slow‑mo, particle explosions.
· High contrast – Bold outlines, distinct food shapes.
· Assist mode (casual) –
  · Auto‑turn off walls (wrap‑around).
  · Show predicted next food location (blinking circle).
  · Undo last move (one per game).
· Left‑hand mode – Flip UI, move virtual D‑pad to left side.
· Font scaling – All text respects system font size.
· Battery saver – Low‑quality particles, reduced frame rate (30fps), disable bloom.

---

7. ANALYTICS & FEEDBACK LOOPS (for players & devs)

· Post‑game screen –
  · Path heatmap: where snake spent most time.
  · Efficiency: average distance travelled between foods.
  · Reaction map: swipes per second, late vs early turns.
  · Tip: “You turned too late 3 times – try swiping earlier when approaching wall.”
· Live calibration – “Swipe sensitivity test” – draw a line, game sets sensitivity to match your natural speed.
· Replay – Watch your last run with ghost trail.
· Crash log – Exact frame of death (hit tail at segment 7, direction conflict).

---

8. SOCIAL & REPLAYABILITY (Android integration)

· Google Play Games – Achievements, leaderboards, cloud saves.
· Share replay – Export as MP4 or GIF – auto‑upload to social media with score overlay.
· Daily challenge seeds – Same maze for all players, compare scores.
· Friend ghosts – Race against your friend’s best run (transparent snake).
· Tournaments – Weekend events, time‑limited, exclusive skins as rewards.
· QR code / link – Challenge a friend: “Beat my score on this seed.”

---

9. TECHNICAL STANDARDS (Android performance)

· Engine – Unity / Godot / custom (Kotlin + Vulkan for smoothness).
· Frame rate – Locks to device refresh rate (60/90/120/144Hz) – movement update decoupled from visuals (fixed 60 logic ticks).
· Touch latency – <20ms from swipe to visual turn.
· Memory – <120 MB, texture compression, stream audio.
· Battery efficiency – Pause rendering when game idle, low‑power mode optional.
· Offline – Fully playable without internet; sync saves later.
· Install size – Under 100 MB (expandable assets downloaded once).
· Permissions – None required (optional: haptics, gyro, notifications).

Include daily challenges, Google Play integration, battery saver mode, and assist options (wrap‑around, undo move). Support 120Hz displays and colourblind presets.
