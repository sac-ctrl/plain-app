Here’s the ultra‑advanced, Android‑first prompt for a modern Space Hunter (a revitalised fixed‑screen / vertical shooter – think Space Invaders meets Galaga with roguelite flair).

---

🚀 Game Title: Space Hunter: Nebula Strike

Core Vibe: Tactile, explosive, and silky smooth. Your ship is a precision instrument; every tap fires, every swipe dodges.

---

1. CORE GAMEPLAY & PHYSICS (Mobile precision)

· Player ship – Fixed at bottom 15% of screen, moves horizontally (X‑axis only).
· Movement –
  · Drag anywhere on screen → ship follows finger instantly (zero smoothing, raw position).
  · Tilt optional – gyro steering.
  · Touch zones – left/right screen halves for tapping (accessibility).
· Firing – Automatic primary weapon (e.g., 5 shots/sec) + manual overdrive (tap with second finger for burst).
· Enemy waves – Procedurally generated waves with increasing complexity.
· Collision – Pixel‑perfect hitbox (ship = 24x24px, enemies vary).
· Invincibility frames – 0.5 seconds after being hit (with blink effect).
· Health system – 3 hits base, can increase via power‑ups.
· Score & combo – Kill streak without missing shots = combo multiplier (up to 10x).

---

2. INPUT & SENSITIVITY (Instant, haptic)

· Drag sensitivity – Slider (0 = very slow follow, 100 = 1:1 finger mapping).
· Edge dead zones – Left/right screen edges ignore drag to prevent accidental exits.
· Tap‑to‑overdrive – Double‑tap anywhere = special weapon (if charged).
· Haptics –
  · Light tap on each shot fired.
  · Medium buzz on enemy kill.
  · Strong buzz on player hit.
  · Triple buzz on boss spawn.
· Long press – Charged shot (consumes energy).
· Gyro calibration – On‑device calibration for tilt steering.

---

3. MODERN VISUALS & ENVIRONMENT (OLED, particle heavy)

Player ship

· Design – Sleek hunter ship with animated engine exhaust, rotating cannons.
· Customisation – Unlockable hulls (Interceptor, Brawler, Stealth, Healer).
· Death effect – Ship explodes into debris + slow‑mo + screen shake.

Enemies (15+ types)

· Swarmers – Fast, low health, chase horizontally.
· Tanks – Slow, high health, shoot back.
· Snipers – Stay at top, fire homing missiles.
· Motherships – Spawn smaller enemies.
· Bosses – Multi‑phase, weak points, pattern attacks.
· Visuals – Glowing cores, flash on hit, death explosion with particle fountain.

Background & environment

· Dynamic starfield – Parallax scrolling, nebula clouds that shift colour.
· Planetary overlays – Gas giants, rings, moons – move independently.
· Debris field – Destroyed ships leave persistent wreckage.
· UI – Holographic projection style, opacity slider, repositionable.

Weapon effects

· Primary – Laser bolts with glow & trail.
· Overdrive – Beam or spread shot.
· Special – Homing missiles, railgun, black hole bomb.
· On hit – Impact spark, hit number pop‑up, screen flash (optional).

---

4. PROGRESSION & DIFFICULTY ECOSYSTEM

Campaign / Endless hybrid

· Acts – 5 acts, each with 10 waves + boss.
· Endless mode – Infinite waves, difficulty scales with wave number.
· Boss rush – Beat all bosses consecutively.

Adaptive difficulty (keeps challenge alive)

· After 3 deaths on same wave → slightly reduce enemy fire rate or increase player health drop chance.
· After 5 wins streak → increase enemy density + introduce elite variants.

Meta‑progression (roguelite elements)

· Ship upgrades (currency earned per run):
  · Fire rate, damage, health, speed, special recharge.
· Perks – Choose before run (e.g., “Shield on wave start”, “Lifesteal”, “Double shot”).
· Permanents – Unlock new weapons, ship skins, starting bonuses.

Power‑ups during run

· Health restore, temporary shield, damage boost, slow time, extra life.
· Drop system – Destroyed enemies have 15% chance to drop a power‑up (floating, magnetic pull toward ship).

Daily & weekly challenges

· Fixed seed waves, leaderboards, unique modifiers (e.g., “only sniper enemies”, “infinite overdrive”).

---

5. AUDIO & ATMOSPHERE (Immersive but mobile‑friendly)

· Music – Cinematic synthwave / orchestral hybrid.
  · Intensity increases with wave progression.
  · Boss theme – separate track with dramatic build.
· SFX –
  · Player shot: crisp laser pew.
      – Enemy kill: satisfying pop + low tone.
  · Hit: crunchy impact with reverb.
  · Power‑up: melodic chime.
· Voice announcer (toggle) – “Wave clear”, “Boss approaching”, “New high score”, “Critical health”.
· Audio ducking – Respects other media playback.

---

6. ACCESSIBILITY & COMFORT (Android specific)

· Colorblind modes – 3 presets (recolour enemies, lasers, health bars).
· Reduced motion – Disables screen shake, camera flash, slow‑mo, explosion particles.
· High contrast – Enemies have white outlines on dark background.
· Assist mode –
  · Auto‑fire (continuous without tapping).
  · Larger hitbox for player or smaller for enemies.
  · Invincibility toggle (for story only).
· One‑handed mode – Drag zone limited to bottom half, UI shifted.
· Font scaling – Respects system font size.
· Battery saver – Reduce particles, cap 30fps, lower effects quality.

---

7. ANALYTICS & FEEDBACK LOOPS

· Post‑run screen –
  · Heatmap: where you died (left/right/centre).
  · Accuracy: shots hit / shots fired.
  · Most dangerous enemy type (killed you most).
  · Tip: “You got hit by snipers – try moving constantly.”
· Live calibration – “Drag test” – move finger across screen, game auto‑sets sensitivity.
· Replay system – Watch last run, with slow‑mo and hit indicators.
· Crash log – Exact cause of death (enemy bullet ID, frame number).

---

8. SOCIAL & REPLAYABILITY (Android integration)

· Google Play Games – Achievements, leaderboards (score, wave, accuracy).
· Ghost runs – Race against your best run (transparent ship overlay).
· Share replay – Export as GIF/MP4 with UI toggle.
· Clans / guilds – Weekly co‑op boss battle (shared damage).
· QR challenge – Generate a challenge from any run – friend tries to beat it.
· Daily login – Streak rewards, bonus currency.

---

9. TECHNICAL STANDARDS (Android performance)

· Engine – Unity / Godot (Vulkan backend).
· Frame rate – Supports device refresh rate (60–144Hz), input at native rate, logic at 60Hz.
· Touch latency – <20ms from drag to ship movement.
· Memory – <150 MB, texture atlasing, audio streaming.
· Battery efficiency – Pause when background, aggressive culling offscreen enemies.
· Install size – Under 120 MB (optional HD assets download).
· Permissions – None required (gyro and haptics optional).
· Offline – Full single‑player offline; cloud sync optional.

Include gyro steering option, haptic feedback on kills/hits, dynamic music, post‑run heatmap of death location, Google Play leaderboards, and assist modes (auto‑fire, larger hitbox).
