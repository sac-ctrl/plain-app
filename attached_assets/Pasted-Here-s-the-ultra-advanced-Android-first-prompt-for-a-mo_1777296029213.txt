Here’s the ultra‑advanced, Android‑first prompt for a modern Endless Runner (3‑lane or 4‑lane runner with parkour, sliding, jumping – think Temple Run meets Vector with roguelite progression).

---

🏃‍♂️ Game Title: Infinite Dash: Neon Runway

Core Vibe: Adrenaline‑fueled, one‑thumb mastery. Every swipe is a life‑saving reflex, every run a new story.

---

1. CORE GAMEPLAY & PHYSICS (Instant responsiveness)

· Basic setup –
  · Character runs forward automatically at increasing speed.
  · 3 or 4 lanes (configurable).
  · Controls: swipe up = jump, swipe down = slide, swipe left/right = change lanes.
· Obstacles –
  · Low barriers (must jump).
  · High barriers (must slide).
  · Moving obstacles (side‑to‑side).
  · Falling objects (must dodge lane).
  · Trapdoors, crumbling floors, laser grids.
· Power‑ups –
  · Magnet (collects coins automatically).
  · Shield (1 hit protection).
  · Slow motion (2 sec, 0.5x speed).
  · Boost (speed burst, invincible).
  · Double coins.
· Collision – One hit = run ends (shield extends).
· Score – Distance + coins + combos (consecutive obstacle dodges).
· Procedural level generation – Infinite, with difficulty curve based on distance.

---

2. INPUT & SENSITIVITY (Swipe perfection)

· Primary controls – Swipe up/down/left/right on bottom half of screen.
· Sensitivity –
  · Swipe length threshold (short flick vs long drag).
  · Dead zone in centre to avoid accidental diagonal swipes.
· Alternative controls –
  · Tilt (gyro) for lane change, taps for jump/slide.
  · On‑screen buttons (accessibility).
· Haptics –
  · Light tick on lane change.
  · Medium buzz on jump/slide.
  · Strong buzz on collision.
  · Soft pulse on coin collection.
  · Rumble on power‑up grab.
· Input buffering – Queue actions during jump/slide animations (max 1).
· Edge protection – Cannot swipe beyond leftmost/rightmost lane.

---

3. MODERN VISUALS & ENVIRONMENT (Cinematic runner)

Character & animations

· Multiple unlockable characters (runner, cyber ninja, astronaut, cat).
· Idle run, jump (flip), slide (dust particles), death (ragdoll + slow‑mo).
· Customisable trails, outfits, accessories.

Environments (themes)

· Neon city – glowing tracks, holographic ads.
· Jungle ruins – vines, moving logs, quicksand.
· Volcanic mine – lava pits, falling rocks, smoke.
· Arctic base – ice physics, slippery lanes, snow particles.
· Cyber desert – sandstorms, cacti, heat haze.
· Each theme has unique obstacles and music.

Visual effects

· Speed lines at high velocity.
· Near miss: screen edge glow + time dilation (0.95x for 0.1s).
· Coin collection: particle burst + pop‑up text.
· Power‑up: screen filter (e.g., gold tint for magnet).
· Death: camera shake, slow‑mo (0.3x for 0.5s), character tumble.

Dynamic obstacles (30+ variants)

· Moving logs, swinging axes, laser gates, spinning blades, collapsing bridges.
· Boss events every 2000m – giant monster chasing, must dodge specific patterns.

---

4. PROGRESSION & DIFFICULTY ECOSYSTEM

Game modes

1. Endless – classic, speed increases indefinitely.
2. Stage rush – fixed distance levels (500m, 1000m, 2000m) with checkpoints.
3. Time trial – 60 seconds, most distance.
4. Mission mode – daily objectives (“jump 50 times”, “collect 200 coins”).

Adaptive difficulty

· After 3 early deaths (<500m) → slower speed ramp, more power‑ups.
· After 3 long runs (>2000m) → add moving obstacles earlier, reduce reaction window.
· Dynamic obstacle density based on player’s recent success rate.

Meta‑progression & unlocks

· Characters – 20+ (stats: magnet range, shield duration, coin multiplier).
· Upgrades (earn coins per run):
  · Shield duration, magnet range, slow‑mo length, double coin chance.
  · Extra life (once per run).
· Power‑up improvements – e.g., shield becomes 2 hits.
· Skins & trails – purely cosmetic.

Daily & weekly challenges

· Fixed seed track – same obstacles for all players, leaderboard for score.
· Special modifiers: “no coins”, “only jump obstacles”, “speed doubled”.

---

5. AUDIO & ATMOSPHERE (Adaptive & immersive)

· Music – dynamic electronic / synthwave / drum & bass:
  · Tempo increases with game speed.
  · Layered instruments added as distance/combo grows.
  · Boss events trigger dramatic shift.
· SFX –
  · Jump: whoosh.
  · Slide: grind.
  · Coin: chime.
  · Power‑up: ascending synth.
  · Near miss: sting.
  · Collision: crunch + glass break.
· Voice announcer (toggle) – “Perfect dodge”, “Power‑up”, “Boss incoming”, “New record”.
· Audio ducking – Lowers music intensity during menus.

---

6. ACCESSIBILITY & COMFORT (Android first)

· Colorblind modes – 3 presets (recolour obstacles, coins, power‑ups).
· Reduced motion – Disables screen shake, slow‑mo, speed lines, particles.
· High contrast – Bold outlines for all obstacles.
· Assist modes –
  · Auto‑jump/slide when obstacle detected (reaction time +200ms).
  · Lane highlight – current lane glows.
  · Invincibility (practice mode).
· One‑handed mode – Swipe zone limited to right/left half, UI shifted.
· Font scaling – Respects system settings.
· Battery saver – Cap 30fps, reduce reflections, lower particle count.

---

7. ANALYTICS & FEEDBACK LOOPS

· Post‑run screen –
  · Lane change heatmap (which lanes you used).
  · Reaction time graph: delay between obstacle spawn and your action.
  · Most deadly obstacle type.
  · Tip: “You jumped too early on barriers – wait 0.2s longer.”
· Live calibration – “Swipe test” – perform 5 actions, game sets thresholds.
· Replay system – Watch last run with touch overlay (swipes shown as arrows).
· Crash log – Exact frame: obstacle ID, your lane, input received.

---

8. SOCIAL & REPLAYABILITY (Android integration)

· Google Play Games – Achievements, leaderboards, cloud saves.
· Ghost runs – Race against your best run (transparent character).
· Share replay – Export as MP4 with speedometer.
· Friend challenges – Send QR code to beat your score on same seed.
· Clans – Weekly clan distance leaderboard, rewards.
· Daily login streak – Bonus coins, exclusive skins.

---

9. TECHNICAL STANDARDS (Android performance)

· Engine – Unity / Godot (Vulkan / OpenGL ES 3.1).
· Frame rate – 60/90/120/144Hz support; game logic 60Hz, rendering at device refresh.
· Touch latency – <20ms from swipe to action.
· Memory – <200 MB (streaming assets for themes).
· Battery efficiency – Pause when background, dynamic resolution scaling.
· Install size – Under 150 MB (optional HD assets).
· Permissions – None (gyro optional).
· Offline – Full offline play; sync when online.
