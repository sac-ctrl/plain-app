Here’s the ultra‑advanced, Android‑first prompt for a modern Color Switch (the iconic timing‑based color matcher – tap to jump through rotating obstacles).

---

🎨 Game Title: Chroma Dash

Core Vibe: Hypnotic, synesthetic, and brutally precise. Every tap bends light itself.

---

1. CORE GAMEPLAY & PHYSICS (Pixel‑perfect timing)

· Basic mechanic –
  · Ball bounces automatically (gravity = 1.2× normal, fixed).
  · Tap = reverse gravity direction (up/down toggle).
  · Ball must touch only obstacles whose color matches its current color.
  · Ball changes color after passing through a color‑swap star or after a set number of obstacles.
· Obstacles – Rotating multicolored bars, spirals, moving walls, zigzag paths, shrinking gaps.
· Collision rule –
  · If ball touches non‑matching color → instant death (unless shielded).
  · Matching color → pass through, add to score.
· Physics precision – Fixed timestep (240Hz collision detection), no clipping through thin obstacles.
· Score system – 1 point per matching obstacle passed. Combo: consecutive matches without touching a swap star = bonus multiplier.
· Power‑ups (rare) –
  · Shield (3 sec, passes any color).
  · Slow motion (2 sec, game speed 0.5x).
  · Magnet (attracts nearby stars).

---

2. INPUT & SENSITIVITY (Tap perfection)

· Primary control – Tap anywhere on screen → reverse gravity.
· Sensitivity –
  · Tap debounce time (prevents double‑tap accidental flips).
  · Optional hold‑to‑float (instead of tap toggle, hold reverses gravity while pressed).
· Haptics –
  · Light tap on gravity flip.
  · Medium buzz on color change (star pickup).
  · Strong buzz on death.
  · Soft pulse on passing a matching obstacle.
· Edge detection – Ball near top/bottom triggers automatic warning (subtle screen edge glow).
· Input buffering – Tap within 50ms before landing on obstacle is still accepted.

---

3. MODERN VISUALS & ENVIRONMENT (Neon dreamscape)

Ball design

· Glowing orb with inner core, pulsing to music.
· Color shift animation – smooth gradient transition, not instant snap.
· Trail – color‑matched particle trail that fades.
· Death effect – shatter into colored shards, screen desaturates then resets.

Obstacles (30+ patterns)

· Classic – rotating colored spokes.
· Moving – horizontal/vertical color bars that slide.
· Spiral – inward‑outward color rings.
· Color wave – undulating path where color changes along length.
· Boss patterns – complex, multi‑segment obstacles with timing windows.
· Visuals – neon glow, subtle rotation blur, particle edges.

Background & environments

· Themes – Unlockable:
  · Synthwave grid – pulsing lines.
  · Cosmic void – stars, nebulae.
  · Abstract geometry – rotating 3D shapes.
  · Paper cutout – flat design.
· Dynamic color – Background hue shifts subtly with ball’s color.
· Parallax layers – Foreground, mid, background move at different speeds.

Effects & juice

· On matching obstacle: screen flash (color of ball), ring ripple from impact point.
· On color swap: particle burst, screen tint shift.
· On death: slow‑mo 0.2x for 0.3s, camera shake, color drain.
· Combo meter – visual flames around ball, screen edge pulsing.

---

4. PROGRESSION & DIFFICULTY ECOSYSTEM

Game modes

1. Classic – Endless, increasing speed and obstacle complexity.
2. Level pack – 100 hand‑crafted levels, each with checkpoints.
3. Time attack – 60 seconds, most passes wins.
4. Daily shuffle – Random obstacle sequence, same for all players.
5. Zen mode – No death, practice timing.

Adaptive difficulty (keeps flow state)

· After 3 deaths on same obstacle → slight slowdown or increase color star frequency.
· After 5 successful passes in a row → increase rotation speed or add extra obstacle segment.
· Hidden “flow score” – adjusts spawn rate of complex patterns based on reaction time.

Meta‑progression & unlocks

· Ball skins – 20+ (fire, ice, neon, chrome, glass, pixel, hologram) – purely visual.
· Trail effects – Sparkles, smoke, rainbow, void.
· Themes – backgrounds unlockable via achievements.
· Upgrades (earn stars per run):
  · Shield duration, slow‑mo length, magnet range.
  · Starting combo multiplier, extra life (once per run).

Daily & weekly challenges

· Fixed seed obstacle courses – leaderboards for score.
· Special modifiers: “invisible ball”, “gravity increases over time”, “only red obstacles”.

5. AUDIO & ATMOSPHERE (Synesthetic core)

· Music – Adaptive electronic / ambient:
  · BPM matches game speed (speed increases → faster tempo).
  · Instrument layers added as combo grows.
  · Color change triggers a harmonic chord shift.
· SFX –
  · Tap: soft synthetic pop.
  · Pass obstacle: chime in key of current color (red = C, green = E, blue = G, etc.).
  · Color swap: ascending arpeggio.
  · Death: descending dissonant tone + static.
· Voice announcer (toggle) – “Perfect”, “Swap”, “Combo”, “New best”.
· Audio ducking – Lowers music intensity during pause / menus.

6. ACCESSIBILITY & COMFORT (Android first)

· Colorblind modes – 4 presets:
  · Protanopia/deuteranopia/tritanopia + monochrome (shape‑based cues).
  · Add symbols on obstacles (circle = red, square = blue, triangle = green).
· Reduced motion – Disables screen shake, slow‑mo, particle explosions.
· High contrast – Bold outlines on obstacles, ball has halo.
· Assist modes –
  · Auto‑flip (game reverses gravity for you at optimal time).
  · Show path prediction (ghost ball ahead).
  · Slower base speed.
· One‑handed mode – Tap zone limited to bottom half or right half.
· Font scaling – Respects system settings.
· Battery saver – Cap 30fps, reduce glow effects, lower particle count.
