What you need to do once on your phone (after installing the new APK)
Open Web Console settings → Always-On Mode, then:

Disable battery optimization — tap, allow.
Enable Accessibility Service — tap, find "PlainApp" in the list, toggle on.
Enable Keep-Alive VPN — tap the switch, accept the system VPN prompt. Then tap "Open VPN settings", find PlainApp, tap the gear icon, turn on Always-on VPN and Block connections without VPN.
Enable Device Admin — tap, accept the system prompt.
Leave the 5-minute watchdog switch ON (default).
Then scroll down and follow the brand-specific section for your phone (Xiaomi/Realme/Oppo/Vivo/OnePlus/Samsung). Those are the manual steps no API can do — autostart toggle, lock-card-in-recents, "Unrestricted" battery, etc. The screen has the exact path for each brand.

After all of that: open the Cloudflare Tunnel page, make sure it shows "Connected", swipe PlainApp out of recents, wait 30 seconds, open phone.shakti.buzz — it should still load. That's the proof it's working.
