Here’s the ultra‑advanced, Android‑first prompt for a modern Dino Run (the classic Chrome offline runner – now with depth, progression, and juicy feedback).

---

🦖 Game Title: Dino Dash: Extinction Run

Core Vibe: Fast, minimalist, but deep. One tap to survive, endless desert ahead.

---

1. CORE GAMEPLAY & PHYSICS (Snappy & fair)

· Basic setup –
  · Dino runs automatically from left to right (fixed horizontal speed, increases over time).
  · Tap or press anywhere → jump.
  · Long press (or second tap mid‑air) → double jump (optional upgrade).
  · Crouch by swiping down or pressing a button (optional).
· Obstacles –
  · Cacti (must jump).
  · Pterodactyls (must duck or jump over, variable heights).
  · Small rocks (can be jumped or ignored with crouch).
  · Moving obstacles (e.g., swinging vines in jungle theme).
· Collision – One hit = death (unless shield power‑up is active).
· Score – Distance traveled + coins (optional) + combo (consecutive obstacle clears).
· Speed curve – Starts at comfy pace, accelerates up to a maximum (exponential fade).
· Day/night cycle – Visual only (no gameplay change), cycles every 500m.

---

2. INPUT & SENSITIVITY (One‑thumb mastery)

· Primary control – Tap anywhere on bottom half of screen → jump.
  · Double tap within 200ms → double jump (if unlocked).
  · Swipe down → crouch.
  · Swipe up (while crouching) → stand.
· Sensitivity –
  · Jump buffer window: tap up to 100ms before landing still triggers jump.
  · Crouch swipe length threshold (short flick vs long drag).
· Haptics –
  · Light tick on jump.
  · Medium buzz on double jump.
  · Strong buzz on collision.
  · Soft pulse on coin collection.
  · Rumble on speed milestone (e.g., 1000m).
· Edge protection – Prevents accidental screen edge swipes from registering as crouch.
· Input buffering – Queue jump during crouch animation (max 1).

---

3. MODERN VISUALS & ENVIRONMENT (Minimalist but juicy)

Dino & animations

· Dino – Smooth, stylised, with run cycle, jump (legs tuck), crouch (head down).
· Skins – Unlockable: classic, cyber, bone, lava, ice, gold.
· Death effect – Dino turns into skeleton or dust cloud + screen shake.

Environments (themes)

· Desert – default, cacti, sandstorms (visual only).
· Jungle – vines, moving logs, rain.
· Volcano – lava pools, falling embers.
· Ice Age – slippery visual, snow, ice spikes.
· Moon – low gravity visual (cosmetic only).

Obstacles & juice

· Cacti – break apart on collision.
· Pterodactyls – flap wings, shadow on ground.
· Coins – float, spin, attract with magnet.
· Effects –
  · Landing dust puff.
  · Near miss: screen edge glow.
  · Coin collection: particle burst.
  · Speed lines when above 80% max speed.

Background

· Parallax scrolling: clouds, far mountains, ground details.
· Day/night transition with smooth tinting.

---

4. PROGRESSION & DIFFICULTY ECOSYSTEM

Game modes

1. Classic – Endless, increasing speed.
2. Time trial – 60 seconds, most distance.
3. Boss run – Every 2000m, a boss obstacle (e.g., giant pterodactyl with pattern).
4. Mission mode – Daily objectives (“jump 50 times”, “collect 100 coins”).

Adaptive difficulty

· After 3 early deaths (<500m) → slower initial speed ramp, more coins.
· After 3 long runs (>2000m) → introduce moving obstacles earlier, decrease reaction window.
· Personalised obstacle density based on player’s recent success.

Meta‑progression & unlocks

· Power‑ups (earned via runs or daily rewards):
  · Shield (1 hit).
  · Magnet (coin attract).
  · Slow motion (2 sec).
  · Double coins.
· Upgrades (permanent, purchased with coins):
  · Double jump unlock.
  · Shield duration increase.
  · Coin magnet range.
· Skins & trails – cosmetic only.
· Pet companions – follow behind, collect coins (visual only).

Daily & weekly challenges

· Fixed seed obstacle sequence – same for all players.
· Leaderboards: farthest distance, most coins, fastest to 1000m.

---

5. AUDIO & ATMOSPHERE (Retro‑modern)

· Music – Chiptune / synthwave, tempo increases with game speed.
  · Layers added as distance/combo grows.
  · Boss run triggers dramatic shift.
· SFX –
  · Jump: boing.
  · Double jump: higher pitch.
  · Crouch: slide.
  · Coin: chime.
  · Near miss: sting.
  · Collision: crunch + game over tone.
· Voice announcer (toggle) – “Good”, “Perfect”, “Boss incoming”, “New record”.
· Audio ducking – Respects background music.

---

6. ACCESSIBILITY & COMFORT (Android first)

· Colorblind modes – 3 presets (recolour obstacles, coins).
· Reduced motion – Disables screen shake, speed lines, dust particles.
· High contrast – Obstacles have bold outlines.
· Assist modes –
  · Auto‑jump (jumps automatically when obstacle is close).
  · Larger collision forgiveness (10% larger hitbox for dino).
  · Invincibility (practice).
· One‑handed mode – Tap zone limited to bottom half, UI shifted.
· Font scaling – Respects system settings.
· Battery saver – Cap 30fps, reduce particles, simpler backgrounds.

---

7. ANALYTICS & FEEDBACK LOOPS

· Post‑run screen –
  · Obstacle heatmap: where you died (cactus vs pterodactyl vs rock).
  · Reaction time graph (obstacle spawn → your jump/crouch).
  · Tip: “You jumped too early for high pterodactyls – wait 0.1s longer.”
· Live calibration – “Jump test” – hit moving obstacles; game adjusts jump buffer window.
· Replay system – Watch last run with touch overlay.
· Stat tracking – Longest run, total jumps, accuracy per obstacle type.

---

8. SOCIAL & REPLAYABILITY (Android integration)

· Google Play Games – Achievements, leaderboards, cloud saves.
· Ghost runs – Race your best run (transparent dino).
· Share replay – Export as MP4.
· Friend challenges – Send QR code for same obstacle seed.
· Daily tournaments – Ranked by distance.

---

9. TECHNICAL STANDARDS (Android performance)

· Engine – Unity / Godot (Vulkan).
· Frame rate – 60/90/120/144Hz support; game logic 60Hz, rendering at device refresh.
· Touch latency – <20ms from tap to jump.
· Memory – <100 MB.
· Battery efficiency – Pause when background, dynamic resolution.
· Install size – Under 50 MB.
· Permissions – None (haptics optional).
· Offline – Fully offline; cloud sync optional.
